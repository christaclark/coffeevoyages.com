About Us

About our mission

Coffee is something we drink every morning, but have you ever wanted to dive deeper and understand where it’s really coming from?

We are entrepreneurs, coffee aficionados, CEO’s, wanderers, all on a quest to find the best coffee. Why not join Coffee Travelers on a life-changing adventure to some of the most remote, wild and beautiful parts of the world? We visit rural, organic, fair-trade farms to give you the most authentic experience with coffee on the planet.

Dave Manzer

Dave is so freakishly into coffee and espresso in particular that he named his business corporate name PR over Coffee Enterprises LLC. When he’s not sipping exotic single-origin espresso or blends from high-end coffee roasters, he’s helping his clients get tons of favorable publicity in media outlets like USA Today, TechCrunch, Huffington Post and CNN. His communications agency, Dave Manzer PR & Marketing, is based in Austin, Texas.

Joel Shuler

Joel is the founder and owner of Casa Brasil, an importer and roaster of small-batch, premium Brazilian coffee in Austin, Texas. He was the first person in Texas to become a Q Grader, a special cupping certification held by only a handful of coffee experts in the world. Joel is currently pursuing his master’s degree in agricultural engineering in Brazil at the world’s most prestigious coffee university, Universidade Federal de Lavras.
